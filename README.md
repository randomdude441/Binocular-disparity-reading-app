# Binocular-disparity-reading-app

Highlight one word at a time to follow along with and test the limits of your reading speed.
Colors part-of-speech like nouns, verbs, and adjectives differently to increase comprehension.
shows two duplicates of the text next to each other that allows the reader to look through the screen as though they were looking at something far away. This reduces eyestrain by preventing the eyes from pointing inwards for long periods of time while focussing on something close up.
